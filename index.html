Dolphins

Introduction to Dolphins

Dolphins are marine mammals that belong to the cetacean family. These animals are known for their intelligence, sociability, and acrobatics in the water. They are fascinating creatures that have captivated human attention for centuries.

Characteristics of Dolphins

  • Intelligence
    • Advanced communication
    • Tool use

  • Adaptability
    • Variety of habitats
    • Ability to migrate

Interesting Facts

  1. Dolphins can live more than 40 years.
    • Some species may live even longer.
    • The oldest known dolphin lived to be 61 years old.

  2. There are over 40 species of dolphins.
    • Bottlenose dolphin is the most well-known.
    • Some species are endangered, such as the Vaquita.

  3. Some dolphins can swim at speeds of up to 60 km/h.
    • They can maintain high speeds for short distances.
    • Speed helps them evade predators.
